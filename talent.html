Trust Has a Voice

Creators are not ad inventory. They are people who have earned permission to influence a community.

That permission is valuable precisely because it is fragile. The wrong partnership can spend years of trust in a single post.

EMG Talent helps creators build businesses without flattening what made people care about them. We identify partnerships that fit their audience, package the opportunity clearly, manage the commercial work, and connect creator insight into the larger EMG ecosystem.

For brands, that means creator work grounded in relevance, not follower counts alone. For talent, it means infrastructure that protects the relationship while creating room to grow.

Before You Sign

Common questions from creators and brands.

What working with EMG Talent actually looks like, in plain terms.

How does EMG Talent's fee work?

EMG Talent works on a commission basis tied to the deals it brings in, with no upfront retainer. You do not pay to be represented.

What does EMG expect from a creator before signing?

An established, engaged audience and a clear sense of who that audience is. EMG Talent is built around representing creators who already have real trust, not building an audience from zero.

How are creators matched with brand campaigns?

By fit, not just reach. Audience, values, voice, and format all have to make sense together before a campaign gets brought to a creator.

Does signing with EMG Talent limit what I can post independently?

No. Representation covers brand and campaign opportunities. It does not take over your account or restrict your own content.

What platforms and creator types does EMG Talent represent?

Primarily TikTok creators today, with the roster expanding as new platforms and formats make sense for the network.
