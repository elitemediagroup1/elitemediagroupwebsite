Engagement Process

From conversation
to compounding.

Five phases. Predictable timelines. Honest checkpoints. The Loop is built systematically — and we'll tell you exactly when each gate clears.

01

Discovery

Before we propose anything, we listen. We map your current funnel, your existing performance data, your conversion economics, and the gap between where you are and where you need to be. This is not a pitch meeting — it's a diagnostic.

We're looking for three things: a clear KPI, a measurable conversion event, and the unit economics that justify the investment. If those three pieces don't exist yet, we'll help you build them before we run a single ad.

⏱ 1–2 weeks
Deliverables
  • Funnel + economics audit
  • KPI & attribution scorecard
  • Vertical compliance review
  • Go/no-go recommendation
02

System Design

We design the Loop configuration for your specific vertical and entry point. Which creator strategy. Which media channels. Which AI follow-up sequence. Which attribution model. Every component is justified against your KPI.

You will see — in writing — exactly what the system will look like, what each component costs, and what the projected outcome is at three spend levels. No black boxes. No "trust us."

⏱ 1–2 weeks
Deliverables
  • Loop architecture document
  • Creative + media brief
  • AI sequence specifications
  • 3-tier projection model
  • Compliance protocol
03

Pilot Launch

Limited spend. Real campaigns. Real data. Pilot phase is structured to produce statistically meaningful signal as fast as possible — typically within two to three weeks of launch — without committing to scale spend until the system has proven itself.

We set explicit pass/fail thresholds for each component before launch. By the end of the pilot, the data tells us whether to scale, refine, or rebuild — and we share that read with you honestly.

⏱ Week 3–4
Deliverables
  • Live creative + media launch
  • AI follow-up live
  • Attribution dashboard active
  • Daily monitoring + weekly reads
  • Pass/fail signal report
04

Optimize & Learn

Ongoing optimization is where most agencies coast and most systems decay. We treat it as the most important phase. Every week, we identify the highest-leverage improvement — creative, channel, funnel, follow-up — and ship it.

Cohort analysis. Creative fatigue tracking. Conversion rate by source. AI message performance. The system is constantly learning, and so are we. You see every read.

⏱ Ongoing — weekly cadence
Deliverables
  • Weekly attribution reports
  • Bi-weekly creative refreshes
  • Monthly system audits
  • Cohort + LTV analysis
  • Quarterly strategy reviews
05

Scale

Only after the unit economics are proven do we scale spend. This isn't a fixed timeline — it's a KPI gate. When CPA is stable, conversion rates are predictable, and downstream metrics confirm quality, we open the spigot.

Scaling adds new channels, expands creator networks, deepens AI automation, and stress-tests funnels at higher volume. The Loop compounds — every layer added makes the next one more effective.

⏱ When KPIs are proven
Deliverables
  • Channel expansion plan
  • Creator network growth
  • Advanced AI sequences
  • Multi-funnel architecture
  • Compounding attribution
Engagement Models

Structured for accountability.

Three ways to work with us. Each one is built so we win when you win — and pause when we shouldn't be running spend.

Performance-Only

Performance-Only

We get paid per qualified lead, booked appointment, or closed transaction. Zero fixed retainer. Maximum alignment. We only make money when the Loop is producing.

Best for

High-volume verticals with clear unit economics: home services, insurance, legal intake.

Hybrid

Hybrid

A lean operational retainer that covers system management, plus performance fees tied to defined outcomes. Stability for both sides, with upside aligned to KPIs.

Best for

Mid-market brands and franchise systems that want consistent operational rigor with performance teeth.

Strategic Partner

Strategic Partner

Embedded, multi-vertical or multi-brand engagement. We function as your performance marketing operating system, with deep integration into your data stack and revenue team.

Best for

National brands, portfolio companies, or PE-backed organizations rolling up multiple revenue lines.

Common Questions

What people actually ask.

How long until we see results?

Pilot launches typically produce statistically meaningful signal within 2–3 weeks. Whether that signal points to scale, refine, or rebuild depends on your vertical, your conversion economics, and the entry point we chose. We share the read honestly either way.

What does pricing actually look like?

Pricing depends on the engagement model and the scope of the Loop configuration. Performance-only pricing is per outcome (per lead, per call, per appointment, per closed deal). Hybrid retainers typically range from a mid-five to a six-figure monthly operational fee plus performance triggers. We share specific pricing after the discovery conversation — never before.

How do you handle compliance in regulated verticals?

Every regulated vertical we serve has a pre-built compliance protocol: TCPA opt-in standards, FTC creator disclosure requirements, HIPAA-aware data handling, state-specific advertising rules, and category-specific creative review. It ships with the campaign — not as an afterthought.

Do you work with brands that already have in-house marketing teams?

Often. The Loop is designed to integrate with internal teams, not replace them. We typically operate the performance media, AI follow-up, and attribution layers while your team owns brand, content, and strategic positioning. Lines are drawn cleanly.

What if it doesn't work?

We tell you. The pass/fail gates at the end of the pilot phase exist specifically so neither side wastes spend on a system that isn't producing. If the data says rebuild, we recommend rebuild. If it says walk away, we say walk away. That's the operator standard.

Can we start with just one service?

Yes. The Loop has five entry points by design. You can start with just AI follow-up on your existing leads, just performance media against an existing funnel, or just a creator program against an existing media plan. The other components plug in when the data justifies expansion.
