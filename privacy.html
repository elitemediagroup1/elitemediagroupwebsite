Privacy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Elite Media Group (“EMG,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This notice explains how information may be handled when you visit this website or choose to contact us.

Information you choose to provide

The website’s inquiry experience submits the details to Elite Media Group through Netlify Forms, the form-handling service provided by our website host. When you submit the form, EMG receives and stores the information and may use it to respond, evaluate a potential relationship, and maintain appropriate business records.

Basic website information

Our hosting provider may process standard technical information needed to deliver and secure the website, such as IP address, browser type, device information, requested pages, timestamps, and security logs. If analytics or other measurement tools are added later, this notice should be updated to identify those tools and the choices available to visitors.

How information may be used

  • To respond to questions and partnership inquiries.
  • To evaluate creator, brand, local, media, data, or strategic opportunities.
  • To operate, protect, and improve the website.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect legitimate business interests.

Sharing and retention

EMG does not sell personal information submitted through this website. Information may be shared with service providers that support ordinary business operations, or when required by law. We retain business correspondence only as long as reasonably needed for the purpose for which it was received, legal requirements, and appropriate recordkeeping.

Your choices

You may choose not to send an inquiry. You may also ask us to update or delete information you previously sent, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Contact

Questions about this notice may be sent to hello@elitemediagroup.io.

This page is a practical website privacy notice and should be reviewed by qualified counsel before public launch, particularly when analytics, advertising pixels, accounts, or Loop data capabilities are activated.
