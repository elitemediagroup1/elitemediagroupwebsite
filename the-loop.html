How It Works

Three moves.
Designed clean.

The Loop turns disconnected activity into an operating system. It captures behavior, connects the data, scores intent, activates follow-up, and improves with every campaign cycle.

Capture

Collect calls, forms, clicks, content source, message response, and campaign context.

Unify

Connect the activity to one contact or source timeline so the story is not fragmented.

Score

Interpret intent, quality, eligibility, urgency, and next-best-action potential.

Activate

Route, follow up, nurture, retarget, book, or suppress based on what the system sees.

Learn

Feed performance back into creative, media, call criteria, and vertical expansion.
