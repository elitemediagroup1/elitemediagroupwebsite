01 — VERTICAL

Home Services

HVAC · Roofing · Solar · Pest · Plumbing

Local intent. High ticket. Competitive CPCs. The Loop targets homeowners at the moment of need — creator content builds trust, performance media captures the click, AI follow-up books the appointment before a competitor can.

The Loop in this vertical:

Micro-local creator content → geo-targeted paid media → inbound call generation → AI follow-up → appointment booked → attribution to source

Compliance Note

TCPA-compliant opt-in flows on all lead capture. DNC scrubbing on every campaign. State-specific license disclosures where required.

02 — VERTICAL

Insurance

Health · Life · Auto · Medicare · ACA

Regulated, competitive, and trust-sensitive. We generate verified, high-intent insurance leads and inbound calls using compliant creator content and media buying strategies built specifically for the insurance compliance environment.

The Loop in this vertical:

Benefit-forward creator content → intent-based search + social → compliant landing pages → live transfer or call routing → AI follow-up on non-converters

Compliance Note

HIPAA-aware data handling. CMS marketing guidelines followed for Medicare. State-licensed agent disclosure on all creatives. No misleading benefit claims.

03 — VERTICAL

Legal & Mass Tort

PI · Mass Tort · Class Action · SSDI

Cost-per-retained-case economics demand verified, high-quality lead intake. We build call-qualified intake funnels that screen for case eligibility before the call reaches a legal team — reducing wasted intake hours dramatically.

The Loop in this vertical:

Awareness content → intake funnel with qualification logic → call-only ad campaigns → live transfer to intake team → AI re-engagement for warm non-converters

Compliance Note

No attorney-client relationship implied in advertising. State bar advertising rules respected per jurisdiction. No specific outcome guarantees in creative.

04 — VERTICAL

Disability / SSDI

SSDI · SSI · Disability Advocates

Empathy-first messaging combined with rigorous qualification. We identify individuals actively in the disability claims process and connect them to advocates through compliant, conversion-optimized intake systems.

The Loop in this vertical:

Empathy-driven creator content → keyword-specific search campaigns → multi-step qualification funnel → call routing to advocate → AI nurture for pending claimants

Compliance Note

SSA advertising standards observed. No benefit amount guarantees. Plain-language eligibility framing only. TCPA-compliant re-engagement sequences.

05 — VERTICAL

SMB

Franchise · Local Business · Service Brands

Small businesses don't need brand campaigns. They need phones ringing and appointment books full. We deliver turnkey performance marketing built for SMB budgets — with the same Loop architecture used by national brands.

The Loop in this vertical:

Local creator testimonial content → Google Local + Meta geo campaigns → appointment booking funnel → AI confirmation + follow-up → review generation loop

Compliance Note

Local licensing and permit disclosures where applicable. Category-specific FTC guidelines followed. No competitor disparagement in creative.

06 — VERTICAL

CPG

Consumer Packaged Goods · DTC · Retail

Creator content that converts at the shelf and online. We build influencer programs tied directly to sell-through data — not reach metrics. ROAS, DTC conversion, and retail velocity are the scorecard.

The Loop in this vertical:

Creator unboxing + review content → affiliate + paid amplification → DTC landing page → email/SMS retention loop → cohort LTV measurement

Compliance Note

FTC influencer disclosure requirements on all creator content. FDA/FTC claim guidelines for health-adjacent products. No unsubstantiated efficacy claims.

07 — VERTICAL

Automotive

OEM · Dealer Groups · EV · Parts & Service

Long consideration cycles and high-value transactions demand a full-funnel approach. We use creator content to build brand preference early, performance media to capture intent, and AI follow-up to convert showroom-ready shoppers.

The Loop in this vertical:

Vehicle review creator content → intent-based search + YouTube pre-roll → inventory/offer landing pages → call or form capture → AI dealer appointment booking

Compliance Note

Dealer-specific fine print and incentive disclosure compliance. FTC auto advertising guidelines. No lease/purchase term misrepresentation in creative.

System Design

Why vertical-agnostic
system design works.

The Loop doesn't reinvent itself for each vertical. It configures. The core architecture — intent capture, conversion, follow-up, measurement — is the same. What changes is the compliance layer, the creative strategy, and the channel mix.

Proven mechanics, new configuration

We don't start from scratch in each market. The performance playbooks are battle-tested. We apply known-working systems to new contexts faster than any single-vertical agency can build from zero.

Compliance built in, not bolted on

Every vertical in our system has a pre-built compliance layer: TCPA protocols, FTC disclosure requirements, HIPAA-aware data handling, and category-specific creative review. It ships with the campaign.

Cross-vertical intelligence

Insights from one vertical make every other vertical smarter. A contact-rate breakthrough in home services gets tested in insurance. A CPG funnel structure improves SMB conversion. The whole system learns.
